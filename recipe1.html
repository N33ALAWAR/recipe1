Marine Biology

The Composition of Seawater

Almost anything can be found in seawater. This includes dissolved materials from Earth's crust as well as materials released from organisms. The most important components of seawater that influence life forms are salinity, temperature, dissolved gases (mostly oxygen and carbon dioxide), nutrients, and pH. These elements vary in their composition as well as in their influence on marine life.

My delicious flafel

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cups
  • i limon juic
  • 2 m.g crushed chickpeas
  • 1/2 tahena
  • 3 stock garlic

Direction

Directions: Servings - 2-3 (about 12 patties) - Falafel mix - 1 cup - Water - 1/2 cup. Servings - 4-6 (about 24 patties) - Falafel mix - 1 box - Water - 1 cup. 1. Combine falafel mix with cold water and let stand for 10 minutes. 2. Pour vegetable oil 1/2 inch deep or more into a deep fryer or skillet. Heat oil so falafel patties fry vigorously (approx. 350 degrees to 375 degrees). 3. Form small patties or balls using two spoons or a melon baller. Fry patties 1 minute on each side until brown and crisp. Place patties on paper towels. Serve while hot. No oil directions: Make 1/2 inch thin patties. Use a nonstick skillet or griddle without oil and cook over medium heat for 3 minutes on each side. Follow steps 1 and 2 above. Make 1/2 inch thin patties and place patties on a nonstick baking pan
